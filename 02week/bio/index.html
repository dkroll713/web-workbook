I didn't discover coding until late into my junior year at college. I enjoyed it so much and understood what we did so intuitively that I decided I HAD to include it in my education somehow. This began with a minor in MIS that grew to become a major as I became more engrossed. Unfortunately (or fortunately!) I realized that I wasn't learning what I wanted to learn in my MIS courses, and decided to pursue further coding education. Since I don't have the time or money needed to pursue a third degree in computer science, I enrolled at the Austin Coding Academy.

My coding plans

I'm hoping this intro course provides me with the necessary expertise to confidently say "yes, I'm good with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and yes I can make a functional website"
